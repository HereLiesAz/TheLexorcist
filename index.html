The Ritual of Automation

Intelligent Scanning

Capture receipts, filings, and other documents with your device's camera. Our OCR extracts the critical data so you don't have to.

Google Suite Integration

Automatically organizes extracted data into a Google Sheet and links all generated documents to your Google Drive. Your case, contained.

Automated Document Generation

Generates cover sheets, affidavits, and metadata pages from your data with a single click, using customizable templates and a powerful backend script.

The Three Steps of Power

1

Configure

Sign in with your Google account and set up your case details. A one-time act of order against the chaos.

2

Catalogue

Scan your evidence. The app captures the data, you categorize it. The machine does the rote work, you provide the intellect.

3

Create

Open your case's Google Sheet and run the built-in tools to generate perfectly formatted legal documents from your evidence log.
